How To Make An Avocado Roll!

A basic and easy recipe to make sushi roles for avocodo lovers!

Ingredients

  • Nori Sheets (seaweed)
  • rice
  • avocado

Steps

  1. first make a batch of sushi rice (or it can be bought)
  2. place a nori sheet horizontally and spread 1/2-3/4 a cup of sushi rice
  3. then flip the nori sheet over and add the avocado slices
  4. lastly roll the sushi and enjoy!
